Sustainability & Women Empowerment

Our argan oil is sourced ethically from women's cooperatives in the Anti-Atlas and High Atlas regions of Morocco. We work directly with these cooperatives to ensure fair trade practices and support the economic empowerment of rural women.

By preserving traditional extraction methods and supporting sustainable harvesting practices, we help maintain the delicate ecosystem of the argan forest while providing steady income for local communities.

Preserving Ancestral Knowledge

The traditional methods of argan oil extraction have been passed down through generations of Berber women. We honor this heritage by maintaining these time-tested techniques while ensuring the highest quality standards.

Harvesting

Traditional collection of argan nuts from ancient trees

Cracking

Hand-cracking nuts using traditional stone methods

Grinding

Stone grinding to create pure argan paste

Extraction

Cold-pressing to extract pure, golden argan oil
